Some of my favorites
Amaebi: Live sweet spot prawns that are made to order with a quick dip in daiginjo to tighten the flesh while adding flavor.
Kanpachi: The fattier, sweeter, and esteemed distant relative of the commonly found, commercially fished, flash frozen yellowtail.
Uni: A spiky carapace intrudes on your enjoyment of their bright orange gonads. This can be found along the coast of Santa Barbara.
Kohada: An increasingly rare quick-pickled fish. Some critics base their entire opinion of the chef based on this one piece.
